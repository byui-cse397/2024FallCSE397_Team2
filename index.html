Idaho Museums

The Idaho State Museum in Boise offers immersive exhibits that showcase the state's rich history and diverse cultures. Spanning over 80,000 square feet, the museum features more than 500 artifacts combined with innovative technology to tell the story of Idaho. IDAHO STATE HISTORICAL SOCIETY The Museum of Idaho in Idaho Falls presents both permanent and traveling exhibits on regional history and science. Notable displays include the "Way Out West" exhibit, which explores Idaho's past from the Ice Age to the present day. MUSEUM OF IDAHO The Warhawk Air Museum in Nampa is dedicated to preserving military aviation history. Its collection includes aircraft from various eras, such as the Curtiss P-40E Kittyhawk and the North American P-51C Mustang.
